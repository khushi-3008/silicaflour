About Silica Flour

36+ Years of Trusted Expertise in Silica Manufacturing

Our story

As a leader in our segment since 2000, Silica Flour has accumulated a wealth of knowledge across everything related to manufacturing dependable silica products. The industry evolves constantly; our long tenure helps us interpret specifications practically—not just on paper.

Our foundation is built on 36 years of hands-on experience in the silica and mineral industry. Under the leadership of our founder, we have developed deep expertise in sourcing, processing, and delivering high-quality silica products tailored to industry needs.

This experience enables us to maintain consistency, understand client requirements, and deliver reliable solutions across glass, ceramics, foundry, construction and allied applications.
