Visiting card

Front and back at 3.5 × 2 in trim with 0.125 in bleed (3.75 × 2.25 in total). For offset printing, export to PDF and convert to CMYK in Adobe Acrobat or your print vendor’s workflow.

Print & production

  • Resolution: Layout uses physical inches (3.5 × 2 in trim). At 300 DPI that equals 1050 × 600 px inside the trim area.
  • Bleed: Artboard is 3.75 × 2.25 in including 0.125 in bleed on each side.
  • CMYK: HTML/CSS is RGB; supply your printer a PDF and ask for CMYK conversion or do it in Acrobat / InDesign.
  • Duplex: Print front and back on one sheet (short-edge flip) or export two PDF pages.
  • Browser PDF: Print this page → Save as PDF → disable headers/footers; set margins to none if available.
