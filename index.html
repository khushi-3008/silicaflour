What we do

Industries served

Glass

High-purity silica underpins batch chemistry and fusion behaviour. We discuss grades against your technical sheets and can align on SiO₂ content and sizing so your formulation performs as expected.

Ceramics

Fine milled silica flour and controlled particle sizes support ceramic bodies and specialty blends. Confirm mesh or micron targets with our team so body properties and firing behaviour stay in spec.

Foundry

Graded quartz and silica sands for foundry and filtration applications where gradation, cleanliness and consistency in the blend matter for surface finish and process stability.

Construction

Industrial fillers and construction-related blends where reliable supply, agreed moisture targets and packaging suited to your site logistics reduce handling friction on the ground.

What we focus on in quality

With 36+ years of industry expertise, we understand consistent quality and strict testing standards. We work with you on parameters that matter, then align batch release to those agreed criteria.

  • Incoming control — Screening raw material against agreed baselines before it enters the process stream.
  • In-process checks — Monitoring key steps so deviations are caught early, not at dispatch.
  • Release testing — Documentation you can file against lot numbers for traceability and audits.
  • Continuous improvement — Feedback from your shop floor informs how we tighten tolerances over time.
