About us

Who we are

As a leader in our segment since 2000, Silica Flour has accumulated deep knowledge across everything related to manufacturing dependable silica products. The industry evolves constantly; our long tenure helps us interpret specifications practically—not just on paper.

Our foundation is built on 36 years of hands-on experience in the silica and mineral industry. Under the leadership of our founder, we have developed expertise in sourcing, processing, and delivering high-quality silica products tailored to industry needs.

Our manufacturing processes are refined through over three decades of industry experience, ensuring precision at every stage—from incoming raw silica through sizing, classification and packaging suited to your line.

What we focus on in quality

With 36+ years of industry expertise, we understand consistent quality and strict testing standards. We work with you on parameters that matter, then align batch release to those agreed criteria.

  • Incoming control — Screening raw material against agreed baselines before it enters the process stream.
  • In-process checks — Monitoring key steps so deviations are caught early, not at dispatch.
  • Release testing — Documentation you can file against lot numbers for traceability and audits.
  • Continuous improvement — Feedback from your shop floor informs how we tighten tolerances over time.
