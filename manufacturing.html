Manufacturing

Experience, process discipline and scale—aligned to your specifications

Our manufacturing processes are refined through over three decades of industry experience, ensuring precision at every stage—from understanding incoming raw silica characteristics through sizing, classification and packaging suited to your line.

We design workflows to reduce contamination risk, maintain lot traceability where required, and keep communication clear between production and your technical team.

From raw material to dispatch

Material understanding

Assessment of feed quality and suitability for target applications—so downstream performance matches what your process expects.

Processing & sizing

Controlled crushing, milling and screening steps to hit agreed particle size distribution and cleanliness parameters.

Handling & storage

Practices aimed at preserving purity and avoiding cross-contamination between grades or lots.

Packaging

Bags, bulk or custom arrangements discussed with you—labeled and prepared for safe transport.

Visits and technical discussions are welcome by appointment. Contact us to align on grade, volume and delivery rhythm.
